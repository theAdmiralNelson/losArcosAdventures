Los Arcos Adventures

Los Arcos Adventures is not a corporate tourism company or a stuffy resort. It was created to help travelers connect with a local, authentic adventure guide. If you want to experience the natural beauty of Puerto Vallarta without the crowds, long lines, and pressuring sales tactics, you've found the right place. So grab a backpack, meet your guide, climb in a boat, and let's explore!

Los Arcos

Los Arcos is a set of rock formations and sea caves just off the coast of Mismayloya (only a few miles south of Puerto Vallarta, Mexico). The formations are home to hundreds of different species of fish and other sea creatures. The snorkeling there is incredible with schools of colorful fish and warm water.
